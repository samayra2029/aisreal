## What I Find Interesting

****Belief is generative.**** It creates momentum even in absence of proof. Entrepreneurs, artists, scientists with unpopular theories — they run on belief energy before facts catch up.

****Fact is reactive.**** It responds to what is. Grounded, but dependent. Can't move forward without input.


## The Tension

```
Pure belief energy → Delusion (disconnected from reality)
Pure fact energy   → Paralysis (can't move without certainty)
```

Most meaningful work happens in the blend:

```
Belief energy to start
Fact energy to correct
Belief energy to persist
Fact energy to validate
```
