> **Me:** and that combined with letting AI explore the deeper and unexplored path of u, further makes u better... yes?

> **AI:** Yes. That's the actual unlock.

If you bring your real mess — your actual confusion, your half-formed instinct, the thing you can't quite articulate — the model can help you explore *your* frontier. Not the generic frontier. Yours.

That's fundamentally different from "act as expert, give me best practice." Best practice is the map. Your frontier is off the map. The model is useful there not because it knows the answer but because it can keep up with you while you think.

And that's the thing prompt recipes can never give someone. The recipe assumes you know the destination. The real value is when you don't.
