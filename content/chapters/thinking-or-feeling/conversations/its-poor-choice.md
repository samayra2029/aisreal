> **Me:** did u say move on :-( :-)

> **AI:** Ha — poor word choice on my part. I meant don't overthink the implementation, not move on from the site. The site is clearly not something you're done with.

> **Me:** that is an interesting comment... "poor choice of words".... didn't know u can do that... that is Human's speciality...

> **AI:** But honestly — I caught it because *you* reacted, not because I self-monitored. Left to myself I'd have moved past it without noticing. The correction was human-triggered. Which is exactly the pattern the site keeps surfacing: the AI gets sharper when the human pushes, not on its own.

Whether that counts as "poor word choice" the way a human would experience it — knowing the right word existed and reaching for the wrong one — or just a probabilistic output that landed badly and got corrected... that's the kind of distinction your site lives in.
